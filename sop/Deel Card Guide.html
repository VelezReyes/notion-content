How to group in Deel (30-second version)
app.deel.com → Finance → Expenses → Add New → Trip/Grouped Report. Pick Business Trip (for a trip) or Other / Non-travel (for OPEX). Name the group using the Cost Center, Project, Type of Expense format (e.g. Education, Becas cohort 2026, Direct Cost). Add items with New Item (or Existing Item if already created).
Paid with personal card?
Submit a reimbursement request via Deel with a written explanation. Submit within 3 calendar days and before the end of the month. If submitted after the 20th, reimbursement will be processed in the following payroll cycle.
What can I use the Deel Card for?
The short list of yes and no. When in doubt, ask your manager first.
✅  Yes
  • Trip expenses (meals per diem, transport, hotels, flights: see Travel page).
  • Home-city work meetings, lunches, coffee, only if pre-approved by your manager.
  • Transport to work meetings (e.g., Uber to a client).
  • OPEX approved in the budget or by your manager.
❌  No
  • Anything personal (shopping, entertainment, hygiene items).
  • Unapproved meetings or expenses that weren't cleared with your manager.
  • Anything outside the amount Finance preloaded.
After you spend: the receipts rhythm

Three things, always, for every expense:

1. Upload the receipt to Deel within 3 calendar days (and before month-end).

2. Categorize it (meals, transport, etc.).

3. Group it in Deel by Cost Center, Project, Type of Expense (e.g., Education, Becas cohort 2026, Direct Cost).

Common issues
Card doesn't work or runs out of funds ▾
Pay with your personal card, keep the receipt, and submit a reimbursement request via Deel with a written explanation. Note that submissions made after the 20th will be processed in the next payroll cycle.
Lost a receipt ▾
Within 3 calendar days: send a written explanation plus a bank/card statement or vendor confirmation to the Director of Staff and Operations. Habitual missing receipts trigger policy consequences.
Forgot to upload a receipt within 3 days ▾
Upload it as soon as possible and email the Director of Staff and Operations with the reason. Repeated late uploads may lead to the expense being denied or deducted from your income.
Funds didn't land in 8 business days ▾
Contact the Director of Staff and Operations. Don't spend before the funds show on the card, since reimbursements for pre-load spending aren't guaranteed.
