Your Deel Card
Every VélezReyes+ employee has a Deel Card. It is your primary payment method for all approved travel and operational expenses. Finance preloads the approved amount before your trip or purchase.

Important · Deel funds
The Finance team funds your card within 5 business days. For travel, funds are loaded in two parts:

• 5 business days after your request: hotel, flights, and travel insurance.

• 1 week before your trip: meals, ground transportation, eSIM, and other expenses.

Card Do not use the card until it is funded ▾

Wait for Gabriela's confirmation before making any purchase. Finance processes fund requests within 5 business days.

Billing address
When entering a billing address for online purchases, use the same address registered on your Deel contract.

If the card does not work or runs out of funds, pay with your personal card, keep the receipt, and request reimbursement via Deel. Notify Gabriela and Dharla. Submissions made after the 20th of the month are processed in the following payroll cycle.

Receipts Keep every receipt and upload within 3 business days ▾

After your trip or expense, create an expense group in Deel and upload all receipts. Name the group with the Cost Center, Category, and Type of Expense. If it is a Direct Cost, also include the Commitment Status and Org/Program.

Upload all receipts within 3 business days of each expense and before the end of the month. Once everything is uploaded, notify Dharla and Gabriela so the expense can be reviewed and closed.

How to group in Deel
app.deel.com → Finance → Expenses → Add New → Trip/Grouped Report. Select Business Trip for travel or Other (Non-travel) for OPEX. Name it using the Cost Center, Category, Type of Expense format.

What can I use it for?
The Deel Card covers approved travel and operational expenses. When in doubt, ask your manager before making the purchase.
✅  Yes
  • Business trip expenses: flights, hotels, meals (per diem), ground transport, health insurance, data eSIM.
  • Client meals when hosting VélezReyes+ partners (no per diem limit, must be reasonable).
  • Home-city work meetings, lunches, coffee, or transport to client meetings, only when pre-approved by your manager.
  • OPEX approved in the budget or by your manager.
❌  No
  • Personal expenses: shopping, entertainment, hygiene items, or anything unrelated to work.
  • Any expense not cleared in advance with your manager.
  • Amounts beyond what Finance preloaded on the card.
  • Using the card before receiving Gabriela's funding confirmation.

Common issues
Card does not work or runs out of funds ▾
Pay with your personal card and keep the receipt. Submit a reimbursement request via Deel with a written explanation. Notify Gabriela and Dharla about the situation, including the total amount. Submit within 3 business days. If submitted after the 20th, reimbursement is processed in the following payroll cycle.
Missing receipt ▾
Within 3 business days: send a written explanation to Dharla along with any available supporting evidence (bank statement, email confirmation from the vendor, or payment screenshot). Missing receipts are handled and resolved directly with Deel.
Forgot to upload a receipt on time ▾
Upload it as soon as possible and explain the delay to Dharla. Repeated late submissions may result in the expense being denied or deducted from your income.
Funds did not load within 5 business days ▾
Contact Dharla. Do not make any purchases before the funds are confirmed on your card.
