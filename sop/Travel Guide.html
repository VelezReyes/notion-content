If something goes wrong
Card doesn't work or runs out of funds ▾
Pay with your personal card and keep the receipt. Submit a reimbursement request via Deel with a written explanation. Submit within 3 calendar days. If submitted after the 20th, reimbursement is processed in the following payroll cycle.
Missing receipts ▾
If you have missing receipts, resolve this directly with Deel. Habitual missing receipts will trigger policy consequences.
Trip cancelled?
Notify Gabriela, Dharla, and Cristina so the allocated funds can be withdrawn. You are responsible for contacting the airline and hotel directly to request your refund.
Need Deel Card funds for something else?
This guide only covers business travel. For home-city work meetings, OPEX, team lunches, or other operational spend, see the Deel Card Policy. Same form, same approvers, slightly different process.
