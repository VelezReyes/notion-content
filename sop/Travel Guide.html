3
Within 8 business days
Deel Card Funds
Director of Staff and Operations (if funds don't appear on time)
▾

You don't need to do anything here. Just wait and check your Deel Card balance.

Important
Do not book anything until the card shows the funds. If 8 business days pass and nothing's loaded, contact the Director of Staff and Operations.
4
Card funded
Book
You (Director of Staff and Ops for exceptions only)
▾

Flights: Book refundable/changeable tickets when possible. Luggage allowance: one carry-on + one checked bag.

Hotel: Book within budget. If it's peak season, get manager approval before booking.

Contact the Director of Staff and Operations only for exceptions: unusual destinations, peak-season rates, meeting room bookings, or vaccination requirements.

Extending for leisure?
If you're adding personal days to your trip, clearly separate personal and business expenses. The company only covers the business portion.
5
During the trip (within 3 days of each expense)
Consolidation
Just you, no approval needed
▾

Pay with the Deel Card for every eligible business expense. Keep every receipt (paper or digital).

Upload to Deel within 3 calendar days of each expense. Group by: Cost Center, Project, Type of Expense.

All receipts must be uploaded before the end of the month the expense happened in, even if the 3-day window hasn't expired yet.

Personal card as backup
If the Deel Card doesn't work or runs out, pay personally and submit a reimbursement request via Deel with a clear written justification. Submit within 3 calendar days and before the end of the month. If submitted after the 20th, reimbursement will be processed in the following payroll cycle.

If something goes wrong
Card doesn't work or runs out of funds ▾
Pay with your personal card and keep the receipt. Submit a reimbursement request via Deel with a clear written justification. Submit within 3 calendar days and before the end of the month. If submitted after the 20th, reimbursement will be processed in the following payroll cycle.
Flight cancelled or delayed ▾
Contact the Director of Staff and Operations immediately. Airline-caused issues are covered, so keep every receipt for any unexpected expenses you incur.
Lost a receipt ▾
Within 3 calendar days: send a written explanation plus a bank/card statement or vendor confirmation to the Director of Staff and Operations. They will review. Habitual missing receipts will trigger policy consequences.
Need Deel Card funds for something else?
This guide only covers business travel. For home-city work meetings, OPEX, team lunches, or other operational spend, see the Deel Card Policy. Same form, same approvers, slightly different process.
