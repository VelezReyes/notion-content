The Finance team funds your Deel Card within 5 business days of receiving your request. This first transfer covers your flight and hotel. The remaining funds for other approved travel expenses arrive one week before your trip.


Important · Gabriela's confirmations
Gabriela will let you know once the funds for your flight ticket and hotel reservation are loaded on your Deel Card. The remaining amount (meals, transportation, health insurance, and any other approved expenses) is loaded one week before your trip.x
3
Trip Arrangements
▾

Once your Deel Card is funded, book your flight and hotel within the amounts approved in your Travel Request. Then arrange the rest of your trip essentials: internet plan and health insurance.

What to do if...
  • The flight price exceeds the budget in the travel matrix.
  • The hotel rate is above the approved amount.
  • You have a special dinner or event with external partners.
  • You need to book a coworking space or meeting room.
  • You are traveling to an unusual destination or during a high-cost season.
In any of the situations above, contact Dharla on Slack.
4
Consolidation
▾
Important
Keep every receipt (paper or digital) for every expense during your trip.

I. After your trip ends, create the Business Trip expense group in Deel and name it right away following the format above. Watch tutorial →

To consolidate your trip, create a single expense group in Deel. Name the group right when you create it, and the name must contain the Cost Center, Category, and Type of expense (OPEX or Direct Cost).

If the expense is Direct Cost
The name must also include the Commitment status and Org / Program.

For the exact options available for your team, refer to the Opex & Investments 2026 file in your team's folder.

II. Upload all receipts within 3 business days of returning from your trip.

III.Notify Dharla and Gabriela of the absolute total expenses and confirm that all receipts have been uploaded, so they can review, approve, and close the trip.

if reimbursement is required
If the Deel Card didn't work and you had to pay with your personal card, submit the receipt via Deel along with a written explanation. Notify Gabriela, Cristina, and Dharla about the situation, including the absolute total of the trip. Do this within 3 business days. If submitted after the 20th, reimbursement is processed in the following payroll cycle.

If something goes wrong
Card doesn't work or runs out of funds ▾
Pay with your personal card, keep the receipt, and submit a reimbursement request via Deel with a written explanation. Notify Gabriela, Cristina, and Dharla about the situation. Submit within 3 business days. If submitted after the 20th, reimbursement is processed in the following payroll cycle.
Missing receipts ▾
If you have missing receipts, resolve this directly with Deel.
Trip cancelled?
The airline and the hotel are responsible for any cancellations on their side. If your trip is cancelled by you for any other reason, you are the responsible person to manage the refunds directly with the airline and the hotel.
Notify Finance
Once the cancellation is confirmed, notify Gabriela, Dharla, and Cristina so the allocated funds can be withdrawn from your Deel Card.
Need Deel Card funds for something else?
This guide only covers business travel. For home-city work meetings, OPEX, team lunches, or other operational expenses, see the Deel Card Policy. Same form, same approvers, slightly different process.
